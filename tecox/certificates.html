Quality Certifications

TECOX Industries is committed to maintaining the highest quality standards in all our products. We are proud to hold the following certifications:

ISO 9001:2015

Certified for Quality Management Systems, demonstrating our commitment to consistent quality and customer satisfaction. This certification ensures that we maintain an effective quality management system across all our operations.

CE Marking

Our products comply with European health, safety, and environmental protection standards. The CE mark indicates conformity with EU legislation and enables the free movement of products within the European market.

RoHS Compliance

Restriction of Hazardous Substances directive compliance, ensuring our products are environmentally friendly. This certification confirms our products are free of hazardous substances like lead, mercury, and cadmium.
