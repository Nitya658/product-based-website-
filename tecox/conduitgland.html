Polyamide Conduit Glands

High-quality polyamide conduit glands designed for secure cable entry in electrical enclosures and panels. Available in both PG thread (DIN 40430) and Metric thread variants to suit various installation requirements.

TECHNICAL DETAILS

Material: Polyamide PA6 V2
Connection Thread: PG (DIN 40430) & Metric
Color: Black
Temperature Range: -30°C to +80°C
Features: UV Resistance (Black), Easy to Assemble, Halogen Free
