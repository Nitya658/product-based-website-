About TECOX Industries

Specialized in polyamide flexible conduit pipes, conduit glands, clamps, cable ties, and drag chains

Our Mission & Vision

Guiding principles that drive our operations and growth

TECOX Industries stands as a prominent manufacturer and supplier of high-quality products specializing in nylon polyamide flexible conduit pipes, conduit glands, conduit clamps, cable ties, and drag chains. We are committed to delivering products that ensure safety, durability, and efficiency in electrical installations.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide innovative and reliable cable management products that meet the evolving needs of our clients. We strive to maintain the highest standards of quality, ensuring that our products offer exceptional performance and longevity.

Our Products

At TECOX Industries, we offer a diverse range of products designed to cater to various industrial applications:

  • Polyamide Flexible Pipes: Including high-grade, slitted, spiral, UV-resistant, and corrugated variants
  • Conduit Glands: Ensuring secure and efficient cable connections
  • Conduit Clamps: Providing robust support for conduit installations
  • Cable Ties: Durable and reliable for bundling and securing cables
  • Drag Chains: Facilitating smooth movement of cables in dynamic applications

Our products are crafted using premium-quality nylon and other raw materials, granting them high flexibility and self-extinguishing properties.

Quality Assurance

Quality is at the core of our operations. Each product undergoes rigorous quality assurance tests to ensure compliance with industry standards. Our commitment to excellence has earned us recognition and certifications from reputed organizations.

Our Vision

To become the most trusted and preferred brand in cable management solutions across India and global markets. We aim to achieve this through continuous innovation, sustainable practices, and by fostering long-term relationships with our customers and partners.

Quality Commitment

Every product that leaves our facility undergoes rigorous quality checks to ensure it meets international standards and our own high benchmarks. Our ISO 9001:2015 certification is a testament to our quality management systems.

Business Operations

The business operations of TECOX industries are guided by a set of core values that emphasize customer satsfaction , quality , innovation and sustainability . Some of the details about our business operations are as follows :
  • Our company was established in 2011.
  • We operate as a manufacturer, supplier, trading company, and wholesaler.
  • Our main domestic market is All India, and we are looking to expand international relationships.
  • We have a production capacity of up to 10,000 meters per day for some products.
  • We have a team of around 25 staff, including designers.
