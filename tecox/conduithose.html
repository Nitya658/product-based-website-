Conduit Glands

Polyamide Conduit Hose Gland

High-quality polyamide conduit hose glands designed for flexible and secure cable entry in electrical enclosures. These glands provide excellent strain relief and protection for cables in demanding industrial environments.

TECHNICAL DETAILS

Material: Polyamide PA6 V2
Thread Type: PG Thread (DIN 40430)
Color: Black
Temperature Range: -30°C to +80°C
Protection Class: IP68 (when properly installed)
Features: UV Resistant, Flame Retardant, Chemical Resistant
Certifications: CE, RoHS, ISO 9001:2015
